Unending Workshop
(2025)

A 3D environment project, exploring a world in which civilisation has been driven by its desire for production within an ever-expanding city. Linking to imagery and ideas stemming from times as early as the industrial revolution, I built upon this to construct an original world relating to concepts of free will, human labour and production present within reality. Developed in Unity and edited with Adobe Premiere Pro.
